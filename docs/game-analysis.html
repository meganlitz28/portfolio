Game Analysis

In Inner Life, when playing the game, they tell you where the Dr is in the “world” and what he must solve to go to a different part of the world. The graphics of the game are two-dimensional and the backgrounds have so much detail in them like the scence when the Dr meets Titan because the mountains have the small details to make it look like it is sand. I have not finished the game all the way through yet, but I believe that the horizon of the game will be the titan finding the others. With all the characters in the game, there is only one character called the Dr that you can control. However, with the other characters, you can only talk to them and not be them. The characters that you interact with are Connie(Leader of Heater room of the heart), Piper(Distribution Center), Titan, and Woody(worker of the legs). When playing Inner Life, the Dr is trying to help Titan work properly for example the Dr went to Piper and Connie to be able to fix Titan's heat source. It would help if you found all the people in the area to talk to be able to do the tasks. When playing Inner Life, there are items that they put in the areas, but you can only pick one up at a time, and you must go back and forth to grab any objects or items you need. for example some of the objects that are in the game are fishs, a glass bottle, a book, and rings. When doing the challenges/tasks, they are self-explainable, but they can be hard because they have somewhat hard obstacles, and they don’t explain what the task is all the way through. You have to fix pipes, find keys to open doors, fix the sink, stop Piper and Connie from fighting, and avoid getting squished by the pressers. Inner Life is a good game to play but the downside to the game that the designers did not add to the game was the save button in the game. So you have to restart the game every time you exit out of it.
