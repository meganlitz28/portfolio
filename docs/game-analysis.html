Game Analysis

In Inner Life, when playing the game, they tell you where the Dr is in the “world” and what he must solve to go to a different part of the world. The graphics of the game are two-dimensional but also have more of a three-dimensional look to them. I have not finished the game all the way through yet, but I believe that the horizon of the game will be the titan finding the others. With all the characters in the game, there is only one character called the Dr that you can control. However, with the other characters, you can only talk to them and not be them. The characters that you interact with is Connie, Piper, Titan, and Woody. It would help if you found all the people in the area to talk to be able to do the tasks. When playing Inner Life, there are items that they put in the areas, but you can only pick one up at a time, and you must go back and forth to grab any objects or items you need. When doing the challenges/tasks, they are self-explainable, but they can be hard because they have somewhat hard obstacles, and they don’t explain what the task is all the way through. Inner Life is a good game to play but the downside to the game that the designers did not add to the game was the save button in the game. So you have to resart the game everytime you exit out of it.
